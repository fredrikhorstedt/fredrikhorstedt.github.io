Rise of China: Timeline

From Manufacturing Hub to Innovation Superpower (1990–2030)

This interactive timeline chronicles the strategic evolution of the Chinese economy, mapping the journey from the early market reforms of the 1990s to the state-led drive for technological self-sufficiency today. It highlights the pivotal moments—in policy, industry, and geopolitics—that enabled China to climb the value chain, dominate global supply chains, and challenge Western leadership in frontier technologies like AI and green energy. Explore how decades of planning have reshaped the global order.
